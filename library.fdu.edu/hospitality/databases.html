  • AtoZ World Business This link opens in a new window
    AtoZ World Business is a comprehensive country-by-country resource for international business and trade. It consists of 100 Country Business Guides of 108 topics each, as well as 76 other world trade resources. Information is provided on business and trade environments, current economic climate, statistical rankings, taxation, business culture, trade compliance for imports and exports, and more. Major contributions from Ernst & Young, The World Bank, World Trade Organization, and GALA (Global Advertising Law Alliance) are included.

  • Hospitality & Tourism Complete This link opens in a new window
    Hospitality & Tourism Complete covers scholarly research and industry news relating to all areas of hospitality and tourism, domestic and international. Its over 933,000 records extend as far back as 1965. There is full text for over 540 publications, including journals and other periodicals, company and country reports, and books. Subjects covered include culinary arts, demographics and statistics, development and investment, food and beverage management, hospitality law, hotel management and administrative practices, leisure and business travel, market trends, and technology.
  • Mintel This link opens in a new window
    Mintel is a consumer research database with an international scope. In over 600 market research reports, it provides data for entrepreneurial studies, sales marketing, and target markets, as well as key current statistical information. Mintel can be used across academic disciplines to retrieve timely cultural, demographic, and environmental trends and statistics that affect international economic conditions.

  • NetAdvantage (Standard & Poor's) This link opens in a new window
    NetAdvantage is Standard & Poor’s comprehensive database of key business information. It provides Corporation records to over 12,000 publicly held U.S. Companies. It includes the Dividend Record to both domestic and foreign stocks, bonds, and money market funds. The Bond Guide is a comprehensive statistical tool to U.S. and Canadian corporate bonds and many of the most actively traded foreign bonds. The Industry Surveys give a current analysis of over 50 major U.S. industries including forecasts, trends and current data. The Outlook, Register, Stock Guide, Stock Reports and Security Dealers of North America are all individual investment decision making tools that add value to the database. Compustat Excel Analytics is also a part of this database with the exception of data being available only for 5 to 7 years.
